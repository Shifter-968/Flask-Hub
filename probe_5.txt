## Main Programmes

![Image 1](https://emrcollege.ac.sz/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/IMG_8497-scaled.jpg)

### Bachelor of Health Science in Emergency Medical Care and Rescue

Bachelor of Health Science in Emergency Medical Care & Rescue is designed to produce an advanced-level health worker whose capabilities enable to provide emergency care services within the Kingdom of Eswatini.

![Image 2](https://emrcollege.ac.sz/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/skills-lab-lectur-9-scaled.jpg)

### Diploma in Emergency Medical Care and Rescue

The programme is a Three (3) year full time course running over six (6) semesters. The program comprises of classroom lectures and practical series of work-integrated learning taking place in both public and private sector in the Kingdom of Eswatini.

![Image 3](https://emrcollege.ac.sz/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/6-12-scaled.jpg)

### Diploma in Occupational Safety and Health

Diploma in Occupational Safety and Health shall be of two years' duration. Assessments shall occur through continuous assessment and formal examination per semester.

![Image 4](https://emrcollege.ac.sz/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/skills-lab-lectur-7-scaled.jpg)

### Certificate in Emergency Medical Care and Rescue

The programme is a One (1) year full time course running over two (2) semesters. Comprises of classroom lectures, practical series of work-integrated learning taking place in both public and private sector in the Kingdom of Eswatini.

### Diploma in Rescue Technology

The course shall be offered for 4 semesters. To train and educate learners into becoming Rescue Technicians who specializes primarily on rescue but are also medically orientated and will be able to provide basic medical assistance should there be a need

![Image 6](https://emrcollege.ac.sz/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/EMRC-2024-NEW-COURSE.-DIPLOMA-DISASTER-MANAGEMENT.jpg)

### Diploma in Disaster Management

The course shall be offered for 4 semesters. Specialized training in the area of Disaster Management in order to offer appropriate advice and services. Skilled graduates will have the capacity to deal with emerging disaster management demands.

![Image 7](https://emrcollege.ac.sz/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/EMRC-2024-NEW-COURSE.-BEAUTY-THERAPY-AESTHETICS.png)

### Diploma in Beauty Therapy and Aesthetics

This diploma program is perfect for individuals who are passionate about beauty and wellness and aspire to pursue a career in the dynamic beauty industry.

![Image 8](http://emrcollege.ac.sz/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/EMRC-2024-NEW-COURSE.-DIPLOMA-IN-PUBLIC-HEALTH.png)

### Diploma in Public Health

The course shall be offered for 6 semesters. They will work with a wide range of equipment's and products. The mission is to provide qualified professionals in the area of Public Health through teaching, research and community outreach.
