About Smart School Hub

Building practical, modern school technology for the next generation of education in Eswatini and beyond.

Who We Are

Smart School Hub is a digital platform focused on simplifying school operations while improving collaboration among students, teachers, lecturers, parents, and administrators.

Founded in Mbabane, Eswatini, we are committed to solutions that are usable, reliable, and locally relevant for both secondary and tertiary institutions.

Mission

To provide user-friendly education tools that improve transparency, efficiency, and accountability across school communities.

Vision

To become the trusted digital backbone for school management and academic performance tracking across the region.

Core Values

  • Clarity in communication
  • Integrity in school data
  • Accessibility for every role
  • Continuous innovation in education workflows
