Section A: General Instructions

  • This form must be completed by all students applying to EMR College for the first time.
  • It is in your own interest to ensure this form is completed in full and that certified copies of all supporting documents are enclosed.
  • If any questions are left unanswered or certified documents are not enclosed it will cause the application to be unsuccessful.
  • For any further enquiries please contact the office at +268 7810 9433.
  • A payment slip (scanned copy) must be attached to your application.

Admission Requirements

  • Consult the college for minimum admission and specific qualification requirements.
  • All candidates who comply with the minimum requirements are still subject to selection procedures.
  • EMRC offers the opportunity for qualifying individuals to apply for Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL). You may apply for RPL if:
    • You are 25 years old or older
    • You have at least 5 years of relevant work experience
    • Your work experience is relevant to the qualification you are applying for
  • NB: Please submit all required information. No late applications will be accepted.

Documents to Submit with Your Application

  • Certified copy of your Identity Document
  • Certified copy of your Form 5 certificate or equivalent qualification
  • If you are still in Form 5, your most recent marks must also be submitted
  • If you attended any other higher education institution — an original academic record and certificate of conduct, or certified copies of certificates/diplomas/degrees obtained previously
  • If the name on your certificate differs from the name on the application form, proof must be provided to explain the change in name

🎓 Government Sponsored Qualifications

  • BSc in Occupational Health and Safety
  • Bachelor of Health Science in Emergency Medical Care and Rescue
  • BSc in Disasters Risk Reduction & Management
  • BSc in Public Health Management
  • Diploma in Emergency Medical Care and Rescue
  • Diploma in Public Health
  • Diploma in Disaster Risk Reduction & Management

International Students

  • Certified copies of your passport / refugee permit / proof of residence / study permit
  • Your school-leaving certificate, submitted with the English translation
